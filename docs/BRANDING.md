## Design Characteristics

- **Predominantly red and white color scheme** - Clean, bold identity
- **Clean, minimal aesthetic** - Swiss/International design style
- **Strong use of grays for hierarchy** - Clear visual organization
- **Blue accents for secondary actions** - Distinct from primary red
- **Warm red (#99211C) as primary brand color** - Core brand identity
- **High contrast between dark text and light backgrounds** - Excellent readability
- **Subtle tints for backgrounds and surfaces** - Layered depth

## Accessibility

High contrast ratios maintained between text and background colors for WCAG compliance.

## Typography Context

Colors designed to work with clean, sans-serif typography typical of Swiss design principles.
